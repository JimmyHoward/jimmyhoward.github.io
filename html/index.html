What I do

Physics and Astronomy Olympiad Training

My expertise is providing Olympiad tutoring to teenagers and aspiring university students studying Physics. My pedagogy prioritises understanding and comprehension of concepts over rote learning, matching the pace of learning of every student. This has been proven to be effective by the 100+ students who gave my sessions a rating of excellent and above during my time at Mathvision Enrichment Centre. Besides olympiad training, I teach the following subjects as well: - Advanced Placement (AP) Physics with Calculus - AT Computational Physics with Python - International Baccalaureate (IB) Physics - A-Level Physics

Robotics and Arduino Lessons

Being the Vice Presient of Nanyang JC's Robotics and Computer Science Club, it was my responsibility to help my members in applying programming concepts to improve their robots. The experience was truly inspiring, as it taught me how to appreciate and respect every individual's diverse perspectives, which I have incorporated in the way I mentor others. Among the many activities I supervised were: - Lego EV3 Mindstorms - Vex Robotics - Arduino Uno Programming
